  • Our Vision

i-Donate aims to be the best blood donation center in serving as a medium in minimising the severe blood shortage due to coronavirus pandemic, to help blood cancer patients and diminish the shortage of blood across India.



  • Our Goal

    • i-Donate's main Goal is to keep track of blood donors, blood banks, blood groups, and blood seekers across India, spread awareness and notify the seekers about every single bit of information on blood related problems and availibility of blood whenever needed.



  • Our Mission

    • i-Donate focuses on saving lives by providing blood and biomedical services through effective outreach to donors, continual development of biomedical expertise, and brand-building partnerships with the community, blood drive sponsors, and health care facilities, Nebraska Community Blood Bank connects those who need blood with those generous volunteers who give.
